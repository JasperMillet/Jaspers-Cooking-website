<h2 style="color:white;">Recipies:</h2>
<h3 style="color:white;">Rosmary Basil Garlic Steak Recipie:</h3>
<h4 style="color:white;">Ingreadients:</h4>
<p style="color:white;">1. Steak(Sirloin Recomended)</p>
<p style="color:white;">2. 3 Garlic cloves skin removed</p>
<p style="color:white;">3. 3 Cloves of rosemary</p>
<p style="color:white;">4. 5 Bay leaves</p>
<p style="color:white;">4. 1/2 a stick of butter(irish recomended)</p>
<p style="color:white;">5. Salt to taste and Ground Black Peper</p>
<h4 style="color:white;">Instructions:</h4>
<p style="color:white;">1. Heat your pan to medium high</p>
<p style="color:white;">2. Season you steak with salt and peper</p>
<p style="color:white;">3. Once pan is up to temp. Placeing the steak in the pan facing away from you. Let sear for 3min.</p>
<p style="color:white;">5. Flip the steak, sear for an additiniol 3min.</p>
<p style="color:white;">6. The add 1/2 of you butter. Then sear for 2min.</p>
<p style="color:white;">7. Flip add the other half of your butter and add your spices. The tilt the pan and baste with a spoon. for an additiniol 2min.</p>
<p style="color:white;">8. Remove from the pan and let rest for 5min.</p>
<p style="color:white;">9. Serve</p>
